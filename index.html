Welcome, and thank you for visiting my personal website.

My name is Bojan, and I currently work at the German Aerospace Center (DLR). I am deeply passionate about science, technology, and all aspects of computing. My fascination with technology and computers has been a constant throughout my life. In my earlier years, I spent a great deal of time engaging with messaging platforms and computer games. Over time, my interests evolved, leading me to explore scripting across various operating systems before I began developing my own website. As part of my journey into computer science, I first delved into HTML and CSS during my undergraduate studies, at which point I acquired this domain. What initially began as a side project in 2017 has since grown into a platform for showcasing my work and ongoing learning. Throughout the years, I have expanded my technical expertise by acquiring new programming skills and working on a diverse range of projects. The primary goal of this website is to present my professional background, share my experiences, and provide insights into the projects I am currently pursuing. Additionally, the Discussion section features topics and papers related to economics, business, and technology — fields I am particularly passionate about.

I hope you enjoy exploring the site. Please feel free to leave a comment or connect with me. Additionally, you can find links to my GitHub and ResearchGate profiles at the top.
