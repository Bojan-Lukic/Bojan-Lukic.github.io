Hi — I’m Bojan.

I work at the German Aerospace Center (DLR), where I spend most of my time thinking about software, avionics systems, and how complex technical systems are built and verified. More generally, I enjoy exploring the intersection of computing, engineering, and science.

My interest in computers started early. Like many people, I spent plenty of time on messaging platforms and computer games when I was younger. Over time the curiosity shifted from using software to understanding how it works — operating systems, scripting, programming, and eventually building things myself.

This website began as a small side project around 2017 when I first learned HTML and CSS during my undergraduate studies. Since then it has gradually evolved into a place where I keep track of projects, experiments, and things I find interesting.

Here you will find a mix of professional work, technical projects, and some of the topics I like to explore in my spare time. The Discussion section contains notes and papers related to economics, technology, and systems thinking — subjects I enjoy reading and writing about outside my main field.

Feel free to look around!
